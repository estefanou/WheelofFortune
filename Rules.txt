  Welcome to Wheel of Fortune
Rules of the Game:
Each round you pick a number 1-5 to choose your category.
    Food and Drink
    Fictional Characters
    Places
    Song Titles
    Phrases
A random phrase within the category will be chosen for you and the two computer players to guess.
Each time you guess a letter you spin the wheel to get money.
When you guess correctly, you will get the money, if you guess incorrectly it is the next users turn.
Good Luck!
